Subject: Letter to new registrants
As you requested, we opened a business number and/or Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) program
account number for you. You can see the number on the attached Business Number Summary of
Accounts.
The summary shows details about your CRA program accounts based on the information you gave us.
If you find any errors or omissions, please make changes on the summary and return it to us in the
envelope provided.
We may contact you to confirm the information you gave us. Having complete and valid information on
file for your business helps us serve you better.
Revenu Québec administers the goods and services tax (GST) for the CRA for businesses in Quebec.
If your business is in Quebec and you need a GST program account number, contact Revenu Québec
toll free at 1-800-567-4692.
The CRA administers the GST/HST and the QST for listed financial institutions that are selected listed
financial institutions (SLFIs) for GST/HST or Quebec sales tax (QST) purposes or both, whether or not
the physical location of the business is in Quebec. If you are an SLFI and you need a GST/HST and
QST or GST/HST account number, contact CRA toll free at 1-800-959-5525.
If you have any questions, need publications, or need more CRA program account numbers, call the
number shown above or go to canada.ca/revenue-agency.
We also offer free seminars for small businesses. For a list of current seminars, go to
canada.ca/cra-videos.
Important: Most important tax notices and other correspondence related to your new business
account will only be available for viewing in CRA's secure online portal, My Business Account. You'll
need to register for My Business Account to access your correspondence and provide a valid email
address to receive notifications when mail is available. Go to canada.ca/myba-sred-service to
register.
Thank you,
Bob Hamilton
